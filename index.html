About PowerHouse Pilates

Powerhouse Pilates is a Classical Pilates style studio that teaches the exercise method that was created by Joseph Pilates. Pilates (Contrology) is a method of training for the complete coordination of the mind, body and spirit. It develops the body uniformly and builds flexibility and long lean muscles, with the emphasis on spinal and pelvic alignment along with breath to develop a strong center or powerhouse.
